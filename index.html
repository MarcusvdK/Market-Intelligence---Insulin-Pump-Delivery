User & analyst views
Beta Bionics — Real-world patient data (N=30,077 sessions)Positive
iLet achieves HbA1c of 8.9% to GMI of 7.4% within 2 days of initiation. >84% of users meeting ADA hypoglycemia goals. Clinical results independent of user engagement level. ~16% operating in fully closed-loop mode. Post-market surveillance May 2023–May 2025.
Insulet — dQ&A EU Patient Voice H2 2025 (n=425) & HCP AID Study (n=414)Positive
Omnipod 5 #1 most requested and #1 most prescribed AID system in the US. >95% of US Omnipod customer base on Omnipod 5; >60% of international base. NCS growth YoY and QoQ in both US and international Q4 2025.
Tandem — Control-IQ+ clinical evidencePositive
115+ peer-reviewed manuscripts, 300M+ patient days of Control-IQ data. Featured 4x in New England Journal of Medicine. ADA Standards of Care now includes AID. Broad clinical validation raises the bar for formulary access and HCP confidence.
Market-level — HCP prescribing patternsMixed
Beta Bionics shows similar glycemic outcomes across endocrinology vs primary care prescribers. Pump penetration in US primary care <10% for T1D and T2D. Current system complexity is the primary cited barrier to PCP adoption.
